Creamy Tortellini Soup

Cozy weeknight Creamy Tortellini Soup
    Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casing removed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium sweet onion, diced
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (9 ounce) package refrigerated three cheese tortellini
  • 1/2 bunch kale, stems removed and leaves chopped
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
    Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in large pot over medium heat and add Italian sausage. Cook until browned and crumble sausage as it cooks.
  2. Stir in garlic, onion & italian seasoning. Cook, stirring frequently, until onions are translucent, for 2-3 minutes; season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Whisk in flour until lightly browned, about 1 minute.
  4. Gradually whisk in chicken stock and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until reduced and thickened, about 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in tortellini, cover and cook until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Stir in kale until wilted, about 1-2 minutes. Stir in heavy cream and basil until heated through, about 1 minute. Season to taste.
  7. Serve immediately.
